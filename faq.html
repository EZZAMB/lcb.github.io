Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take walk-ins?

Yes, we welcome walk-ins. However, availability depends on how busy we are. For guaranteed service, we recommend booking an appointment.

Do I need to make an appointment?

While appointments are not mandatory, they are highly recommended to ensure you get your desired time slot. Walk-ins are served on a first-come, first-served basis.

What happens if I'm late to my appointment?

If you're late, we will do our best to accommodate you, but your service time may need to be adjusted. If you're more than 15 minutes late, we may need to reschedule your appointment.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule your appointment. We kindly ask that you notify us at least an hour before your set appointment time, to make the necessary adjustments.

Do you accept credit cards?

Yes, we accept all major credit cards, as well as cash payments. We also take Apple Pay.

Are you hiring?
